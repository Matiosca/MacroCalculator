How to determine grams ratio

Daily calories intake is the main deciding factor that leads to an increase or decrease in bodyweight.
Equally important are the sources from where we get these calories. The three main sources are called Macronutrients or "macros" and are respectively proteins, fats and carbohydrates.
Basic recommendation for the average adult to maintain weight are:
Average adult: 30 years-old - 2728 Kcal - 1.78m/5'10" - 80kg/176Lb

Proteins 15~20% Fats 20~25%
1.5 g/Kg 0.6 g/Kg
0.9 g/Lb 0.4 g/Lb
These intakes greatly varie based on people training and diet.
